About materi.al

At material, we believe fashion should make a statement—not just in style but in values. Based in Brisbane/Meanjin, we are committed to creating a positive impact on the community and the planet through sustainable and ethical practices.

Our collections focus on using locally sourced materials and giving pre-loved clothing a new life. By blending vintage aesthetics with modern streetwear, we offer unique pieces that reflect individuality and mindfulness. Whether you’re looking for standout styles or timeless classics, our clothing is designed with care and purpose.

We are proud to donate half of all profits to charities dedicated to supporting our community, ensuring every purchase contributes to meaningful change. Beyond our online store, you can find us at thrifting events across Australia, where we source and sell curated collections that celebrate creativity, culture, and sustainability.

Join us in redefining streetwear—one ethically sourced piece at a time. Together, we can build a wardrobe and a world that truly reflects our values.
